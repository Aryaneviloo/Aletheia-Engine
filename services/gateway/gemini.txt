### 👁️ What Makes This "Enterprise-Grade"?

To move beyond the "small" feel, we introduce three core features:

#### 1. The Oracle’s Justice (The Judge)
We will add a "Hallucination Sentinel." When the **Voice** synthesizes an answer, a second hidden worker (The Judge) cross-references the answer against the **Ledger** to ensure the machine hasn't dreamed up a lie. If the score is low, the system warns you: *"The Voice is speculating."*

#### 2. The Multi-Worker "Forge"
Since we are using Celery and Redis, scaling to 3 workers is trivial but powerful. You can have:
* **Worker-A:** Dedicated to heavy PDF ingestion.
* **Worker-B:** Dedicated to the high-dimensional 3D math.
* **Worker-C:** Dedicated to the Voice (Synthesis).
* *This prevents one heavy PDF from "silencing" the Voice.*

#### 3. Universal Accessibility
By using **Next.js** with **Tailwind CSS**, we create a UI that feels like a premium research tool—think a hybrid of Obsidian and Perplexity. It will be fully responsive, meaning you can check your "Knowledge Constellation" from your phone as easily as your desktop.
